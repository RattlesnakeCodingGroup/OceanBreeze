{# Fresh Seafood, Fresh Food #} About Us

Castillo Honey Inc has been in the honey bee industry for over 6 years. We started providing pollination services locally and have provided services for a variety of farmers. From the Almond trees in California to the Strawberry fields of Florida, quality pollination is at the core of our mission. Our goal is to give bees the best quality of life and help boost the ecosystem and crops around them.
